About Me

Being a queer person myself, it's very important to me that knowledge about queer identities and queer issues be spread throughout the general populus. I, myself, am transgender, gender fluid, non-binary, and pansexual. These identities alone may require some explaining for the average non-queer person to understand or even for some queer people to understand. That's where I come in. I'm able to explain the different nomenclature and naming conventions of queer identities and the queer community, as well as the interpersonal relations within the community and all the complexities therein.
